Types of Photography

There are many different types of photography. Dorothea Lange was a photojournalist. She used her art to share information with the world.

Get a flash of inspiration! Photojournalism is an art focused on capturing images of important events. Photojournalists use their art to provide information about what is happening at a specific time.

In 1936, Dorothea Lange took a photo titled Migrant Mother. It’s one of the most well-known photos in the history of photojournalism. The photo shows a worried mother surrounded by some of her children. It shares a story of poverty and hardship that many faced during the Great Depression.
