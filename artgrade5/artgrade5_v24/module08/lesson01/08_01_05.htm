Getting Ready

When an artist decides to enter their artwork into an exhibit, they need to check the criteria. Here’s one my school gave me when I entered my art.

Fifth Grade Art Exhibit

All fifth-grade students may submit artwork for this end-of-the-year art exhibit.

Choose an element of art and a principle of design to use in your art.

Create an original piece of art that shows the skills you have learned this year.

Make sure your artwork is neat and complete.

I’m getting ready for my very first art exhibit. It’s so exciting! There’s a lot to do to get ready. I’m writing everything down so that I don’t miss anything. Take a look and see what you think.

Sometimes, artists create a new piece of artwork for an exhibition. Other times, they choose a piece of art they are proud of that fits the criteria. Take a look at the artwork below and see if this would be a good submission.

Ask yourself:

  • What is the element of design you are highlighting?
  • What principle of design are you focusing on?
  • Is this work neat and complete?
  • Are the artist’s best craftsmanship skills displayed?
  • Would this be a good submission for an art exhibit?

Possible Answers
  • Element of art: color
  • Principle of design: proportion
  • The art is neat and complete.
  • The colors are blended. There is not much white space.
  • This would be a great submission for an art exhibit.
