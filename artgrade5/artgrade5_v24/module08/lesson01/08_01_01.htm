Community Art

Art is in the heart of a community! Join me in discovering how art is celebrated in different communities.

There are so many different types of art found in communities. These are places you might be able to find art in your community.

Explore art in your local museums. Many communities have museums to showcase art. Galleries and community centers also showcase local art, such as paintings and sculptures.
