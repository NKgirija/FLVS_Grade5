Exhibit Expectations

Be prepared! Knowing what to expect can help you prepare for new experiences.

The artist who drew the teddy bear used lines that crisscross to show a soft, furry texture.

Now imagine getting up to the counter to order and being told they only serve hamburgers. How would you feel?

Whether you are going to an art exhibit or a restaurant, knowing what to expect helps you to enjoy the experience.

Most art exhibits have some kind of theme and criteria for the art that is on display. This helps artists know what kind of art to enter. It also prepares people coming to the exhibit to know what to expect.
