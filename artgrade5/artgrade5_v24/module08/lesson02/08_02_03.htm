Changes

Artists are always growing and changing. So never stop developing your art skills!

Frida Kahlo’s artistic style changed over time. It is easy to see the changes in her style when you compare her artwork across the 1940s.

How did Kahlo's artwork change over time?
©Self-portrait with Thorn Necklace and Hummingbird, Frida Kahlo, 2009, oil on canvas
© Diego On My Mind, Frida Kahlo, 1943, oil on masonite
© Diego & I, Frida Kahlo, 1949, oil on canvas

In her earlier work, Kahlo painted herself surrounded by more natural elements, like plants and animals, and wrapped in a green vine. 

A few years later, Kahlo still included a few nature elements, like the flowers around her head, but also added some symbolism into her self-portraits. 

Toward the end of her career, Kahlo didn't include any elements of nature in her self-portraits, and the symbolism in her artwork became the main focus.
