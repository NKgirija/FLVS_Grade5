Practice like an Artist

Practice like an Artist activities are optional and just for you! This activity does not get submitted to your instructor. Enjoy practicing what you have learned.

Gather Supplies

  1. Your sketchbook
  2. Pencil and eraser
  3. Coloring tools
  4. Ruler
  5. Photo or magazine image

Complete the Activity

Grab your sketchbook and a drawing tool. Follow along and create a Grid Portrait.

Select a picture of your choice.

  • Using a ruler, create a grid on your picture and in your sketchbook page.

Draw from left to right one box at a time.

  • Use light pressure when drawing the lines in each grid box.
  • Using a piece of paper to focus on one box at a time might be helpful.

Draw all the lines and shapes you can see in each individual box onto the grid in your sketchbook.

Add more details to each box or revisit and use a harder pressure to darken the lines.

When complete, your finished drawing should be an enlarged version of the original-sized picture.

Add color or more details to your artwork until you are happy with it.

Be Proud of Your Work

Show off your Grid Portrait to a trusted adult.
