Portraits and Proportion

Smile and paint! Self-portraits are in many ways like a selfie in that the artist paints themselves. Frida Kahlo is known for her self-portraits. She took time to plan her artwork using the principles of design.

There were times in Frida Kahlo’s life when she was unable to leave her bed. During these times, she used a special easel and mirror to help her paint self-portraits from her bed.

©Mexican artist. Painting in bed. Juan Guzman, 1952

Kahlo used a mirror to look at herself while painting her self-portraits. Many artists who paint portraits have a model pose for them. Before they begin, the artist chooses how they want to pose their subject. Take a look at some examples of portrait poses by Gustav Klimt.
