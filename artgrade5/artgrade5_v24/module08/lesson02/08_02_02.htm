Meet an Artist

My art was strongly influenced by my life and the community where I lived in Mexico. I hope you enjoy my paintings.

Explore this painting, Self Portrait with Bonito, created by Frida Kahlo.

Public Domain

In several of her early paintings, Kahlo included monkeys. Often, she painted herself holding her pet monkeys, Fulang-Chang and Caimito do Guayabal. Kahlo was very attached to her monkeys and cared for them as if they were children.
