Art with Texture

Hi, artist friend! Welcome you to the beautiful world of texture. I love using texture in my art. I especially like how it’s up to us as artists to choose how we want to use texture in our artwork. Join me in discovering how to use texture!

Watch this video to learn more.

Adding texture to your artwork makes it more interesting for viewers. It brings depth and a sense of touch to the visual experience.

Explore different texture groups.

Actual Texture

Touching an object with your hands allows you to experience its actual texture.

Implied texture creates the illusion of a certain feeling on something flat, which involves employing specific techniques and tools.
