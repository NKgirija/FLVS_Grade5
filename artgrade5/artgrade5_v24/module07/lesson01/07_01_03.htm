Imply Texture

Implied textures are like magic! Artists can create illusions that captivate the eyes but cannot be touched or felt.

Texture in art is all about using your sense of touch and feeling. Your experience of visual texture depends on your previous knowledge of physical texture. Look at this example.

Have you ever had chocolate pudding? Do you know what it feels like? It’s cold, smooth, gooey, and soft. You might even be able to imagine what it tastes like.
