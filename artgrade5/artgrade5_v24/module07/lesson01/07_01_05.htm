Feeling Art

To touch or not to touch! It’s important to be respectful of artwork. Artists can spend their entire careers working toward improving, mastering, and refining the skills needed to complete their artwork. This extreme amount of time and effort is one reason why respecting the artwork that artists create is important.

©

Take a few moments to review how you can show respect for other artist’s work.

Art should be treated like treasure! Handle artwork with clean, dry hands or wear gloves. Avoid touching the surface directly and refrain from using excessive pressure and touching areas that are delicate on the artwork.
