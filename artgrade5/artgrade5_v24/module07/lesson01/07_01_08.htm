Practice like an Artist

Practice like an Artist activities are optional and just for you! This activity does not get submitted to your instructor. Enjoy practicing what you have learned.

Gather Supplies

  1. Your sketchbook or paper
  2. Pencil
  3. Various items with different textures from your home and yard. (Be sure to get adult permission to cut and glue the items you choose for your artwork.)
  4. Scissors
  5. Glue
  6. Eraser
  7. Coloring tools

Complete the Activity

Grab your sketchbook and a drawing tool. Get ready to practice using the element of art, texture! Follow along and create a Texture Collage drawing.

Take a texture scavenger hunt.

  • Take a scavenger hunt through your house to find items with different textures. (sandpaper, cotton ball, etc.)
  • With adult supervision, extend the hunt to your yard to find more texture items. (leaf, stick, etc.)
  • Bring the items to your table with the other supplies to create a texture collage artwork.

Make a plan.

  • Decide what image you want to create. Think about what inspires you. (sports, animals, music, etc.)
  • Lightly sketch your idea out in your sketchbook to guide you.

Cut items you collected as needed. Glue them to create your texture collage artwork.

Use the drawing and coloring tools of your choice to create a sketch of your texture collage artwork.

Add texture and details to your drawing.

  • Use diagonal, wavy, curved, or straight lines to create a leafy or feathery texture.
  • Use short jagged choppy lines for grass, torn paper, or fuzzy textures.
  • Use a light pressure and spirals to create a fluffy or cloudy texture.
  • Use harder pressure to make the shadows or the darkest parts of the texture darker.
  • Use a light pressure or an eraser to lighten the color of the area where the light hits.

Use an eraser or coloring tools of your choice to add details to your artwork.

Be Proud of Your Work

Show off your Texture Collage drawing to a trusted adult.
