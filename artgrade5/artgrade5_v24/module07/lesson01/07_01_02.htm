Actual Texture

Show off your feelings with texture! Artistic texture is about having feelings and engaging your sense of touch. Your perception of visual texture is influenced by your prior understanding of actual texture.

©

Touching actual texture is fun as you can feel it with your hands, typically found in three-dimensional artwork. In contrast, the implied texture is visible but not tangible, as seen in two-dimensional artwork. Artists use actual texture to provide viewers with a visual experience of depth and emotion.
