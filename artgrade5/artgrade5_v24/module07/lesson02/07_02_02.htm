El Anatsui's artwork was created using recycled materials. Anatsui shows how art is for everyone, regardless of a person's socioeconomic status.

Wall Sculpture by El Anatsui by See-ming Lee © 2010 flickr, CC BY-SA 2.0

Anatsui's artwork makes a statement. It communicates a message about the effect of littering on the environment. By creating sculptures from objects thrown away, he encourages his viewers to stop littering and consider other purposes for trash items.

 

Anatsui repurposes recycled materials to create artworks that communicate messages about his culture or community. How can you create your own artwork that communicates a message about your culture or community? What materials would you use?
