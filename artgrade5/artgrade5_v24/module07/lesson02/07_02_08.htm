Practice like an Artist

Practice like an Artist activities are optional and just for you! This activity does not get submitted to your instructor. Enjoy practicing what you have learned.

Gather Supplies

  1. Your sketchbook
  2. Pencil
  3. Coloring tools
  4. Cardboard
  5. Coloring tools
  6. Tape or glue

Complete the Activity

Grab your sketchbook and a drawing tool. Follow along and create a Texture Cube.

  • Step 1
  • Step 2
  • Step 3
  • Step 4

Grab your cardboard.

  • Measure and cut out six even squares of cardboard or thick paper.

Treat each square as its own unique artwork.

  • Use different texture drawing techniques to fill in each of the squares.
  • Practice cross-hatching, stippling, shading, and other techniques using the coloring tool of your choice.

Use glue or tape to assemble your cube.

  • Glue
    • Place glue along the edge of each square.
    • Attach the edges of each square to the others.
    • Allow the glue to dry.
  • Tape
    • Place four squares next to one another.
    • Place tape along the edge of two squares so they're connected.
    • Place one square above and one square below.
    • Bring the edge of the left square to the right and connect with tape to create cube walls.
    • Fold top and bottom squares and connect with tape.

Once assembled, continue adding different textures or details to each side of the cube.

Be Proud of Your Work

Show off your Texture Cube to a trusted adult.
