Trash or Treasure

Sometimes, I save some of the items I normally would recycle and use them as materials to make art. Come, let me show you some ideas how you can do that, too!

Anatsui's artwork shows that old, discarded objects can be used to make something totally new and unique. You can repurpose materials to give them a new meaning, too! Check out how these bottle caps are transformed into a colorful world map.

Use your imagination and think about what you can make by repurposing unwanted materials. For example, you can turn an old pair of shoes into a new, boot-iful artwork!

The best part about repurposing is that you will be saving things from clogging up landfills.
